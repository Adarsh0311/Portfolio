About

Software Engineer with over 3 years of experience in full-stack development, I've had the privilege of building robust and scalable solutions. My journey began with a deep dive into backend technologies, mastering Java with Spring Boot and C# with .NET to create powerful server-side logic and APIs.

On the frontend, I specialize in Angular, crafting intuitive and dynamic user interfaces that provide a seamless user experience. My expertise extends to database management with PostgreSQL, ensuring data integrity and performance. I'm passionate about the entire development lifecycle, from conception and design to deployment and maintenance.

Currently, I am expanding my skill set into cloud computing, particularly with AWS. I'm excited to leverage cloud services to build even more resilient, scalable, and efficient applications. I thrive in collaborative environments and am always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenging projects.
